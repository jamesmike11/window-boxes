Choose Unique Shapes for Boxes and Windows

Being creative means trying out new and innovative styles for packaging, especially when it comes to window boxes. Such trendy boxes need to be crafted in distinctive shapes radiating modish vibes. Typical materials used for custom window boxes, including cardboard, Kraft paper, Rigid, and corrugated sheets, show maximum tendency to be molded to any shape and size depending on the product’s specification. Feel free to design them in round, square, triangle, or rectangle. Besides, reflect your originality with folding, flat, tray, drawer, sleeve boxes, etc.

Coming to the shape of the window, with the help of the latest die-cutting technique, you can get any shape giving an enthralling outlook. The most commonly used and preferable options for window shapes comprise classic, timeless geometric shapes, such as rectangular, triangle, square, oval, or round.

The Options of Edge and Frame Design

Are you selling a luxury product? You need your packaging viewing window to be keenly refined and exquisitely defined. Having an edge design will surely pop your eyes out. If you need to enjoy a high-quality window, we advise you to choose an edge design. But for a sophisticated appearance, finely finessed frames design is your option, and trust us, it will not disappoint you.

Bring a Touch of Aesthetic films

For covering the die cuts in these fashionable boxes, you must look for the different movies and coverings. These are designed to offer protection to the product from outside elements. Opt fluorescent and tinted plastic films.

Quality Always Matters In Window Boxes!

Never compromise on the quality of your packaging. And that is why choosing a suitable material is one of the crucial primary steps. The quality of a box should be supreme to make sure it will last longer and offer protection to your product. And always remember: top-quality packaging creates a positive brand image, building trust. Using quality PVC or durable plastic for windows will be your investment. After all, how do you expect the customers to react to your rough, unattractive, sub-par, or broken window?

Apart from window and packaging material, but focus on printing and lamination quality, as well. Pick ground-breaking printing techniques and pioneering coating methods such as aqueous and spot UV for an authentic premium look. Laminations in either glossy or matte will give your product the most brilliant touch.

Go For Stylish Foiling

One of the most incredible customization techniques is foiling either in gold or silver used for highlighting the critical information on your custom window boxes as well as Sleeve boxes. You can choose a copper or metallic sheet for engraving, too. Another epic way of utilizing the appealing benefits of foiling is to outline the cutouts in a color of your choice. Elegant! It will divert the attention directed toward the window means your product.
